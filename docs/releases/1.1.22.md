# Release 1.1.22 — seal the level 6 teardown breach

Fix the level 6 completion crash by making helper-wing and hazard cleanup survive Phaser internals that have already begun shutting down.

## Highlights

- Lets level 6 complete instead of throwing `undefined is not an object (evaluating 'n.forEach')` during helper-wing teardown.
- Makes last-life helper-wing destroy idempotent across already-disposed colliders, helper bodies, and group internals.
- Extends the same teardown guardrails to enemy-pool child iteration and player-hazard cleanup so other terminal handoffs follow the safer path.

## What changed

- Helper-wing teardown:
  - `src/systems/LastLifeHelperWing.ts`
  - Helper ships and overlap colliders are destroyed best-effort before clearing the group without re-destroying children through Phaser group internals.
  - Destroy and suspend calls tolerate scene-transition teardown that has already invalidated bodies, colliders, or group child storage.

- Adjacent lifecycle hardening:
  - `src/systems/EnemyPool.ts`
  - `src/systems/CollisionManager.ts`
  - Group child reads now fail closed during transition cleanup instead of throwing through level completion.

- Regression coverage:
  - `tests/LastLifeHelperWing.test.ts`
  - `tests/EnemyPool.test.ts`
  - `tests/CollisionManager.test.ts`
