# Release 1.1.21 — stabilize level 6 boss/death handoff

Fix the level 6 boss-helper freeze by making scene-flow transition intent durable and helper-wing sync transition-aware.

## Highlights

- Preserves queued level-complete intent across non-final respawn so boss defeat can still hand off after the player is alive again.
- Makes accepted terminal transitions monotonic: level-complete cannot be overturned by later death events, while final death can still beat a queued-but-unaccepted completion.
- Keeps helper-wing behavior additive in ordinary last-life respawn while preventing helper sync and death side effects during level-complete or ignored terminal handoffs.

## What changed

- Scene flow:
  - `src/scenes/gameScene/GameSceneFlowController.ts`
  - Durable level-complete intent is separate from the zero-delay flush timer.
  - Respawn clears only timer work and retries queued completion on completion.
  - `handlePlayerDeath()` returns a structured outcome with post-death remaining lives.

- Combat/helper handoff:
  - `src/scenes/gameScene/combatFeedbackHandlers.ts`
  - Helper-wing sync now runs only for ordinary respawn with no queued level completion.
  - Ignored terminal death outcomes skip player death animation, explosion, and audio side effects.

- Regression coverage:
  - `tests/GameSceneFlowController.test.ts`
  - `tests/combatFeedbackHandlers.test.ts`
  - `tests/gameSceneFlowContextBridge.test.ts`
