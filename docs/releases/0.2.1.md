# Space Explorer 0.2.1

Release date: 2026-04-07

## Highlights

- Fixed an overly dark scene regression introduced by the Phaser 4 camera grade migration.
- Improved frame pacing by trimming expensive full-screen effects and scaling visual density on lower-end devices.

## What changed

- Mapped legacy level `colorGrade` values to Phaser 4 `ColorMatrix` semantics so brightness/contrast/saturation behave as intended.
- Retuned camera post effects for readability (lighter vignette and subtler global glow).
- Removed global full-screen blur and introduced adaptive quality scaling for:
  - particle emitter reserve and burst counts
  - starfield and scenic nebula detail density
  - debris mote density
