# Release 0.5.3 — Boss durability rebalance

## Highlights

- Boss HP scaling now ramps much harder against upgrade-heavy runs.
- Fire-rate and damage upgrades now contribute substantially more to boss toughness.
- Progression and survivability upgrades also contribute, with bounded scaling for readability.

## What changed

- Reworked `src/systems/balance/bossScaling.ts` from a light weighted-pressure model to a stronger multi-factor model:
  - **Level bonus**: significantly increased late-campaign boss durability.
  - **Offense bonus**: derived from effective player DPS (damage × fire-rate), then level-gated.
  - **Defense bonus**: moderate HP/shield contribution.
  - **Hard cap** to prevent runaway values while still allowing much tougher bosses.

## Why

Players with heavy fire-rate (and damage) upgrades were burning bosses down too quickly. This patch keeps high-upgrade runs challenging without making early runs unfair.
