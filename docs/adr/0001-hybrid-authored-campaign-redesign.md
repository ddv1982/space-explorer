# Hybrid Authored Campaign Redesign

Space Explorer will improve gameplay variety through a hybrid campaign redesign: preserve config-driven level authoring where it works, but add targeted authored mechanics when a resolved level skill cannot be expressed by the existing weighted-random wave model. We chose this over a pure tuning pass because literal cover, authored recovery relief, and signature waves need predictable player-facing structure; we chose it over broad mechanics expansion to preserve arcade readability and avoid roster/hazard bloat.

## Consequences

- Key signature moments may be authored through lane-based templates rather than only section-level weighted random spawning.
- Literal cover is a deliberate mechanic for cover-focused levels: cover blocks enemy bullets and bombs, but not enemy bodies.
- Authored health/shield relief may appear in Recovery Beats when endurance pacing needs reliable stabilization.
- New enemy or hazard types require strict justification: a clear unique behavior, readable telegraphing, and reuse beyond a single isolated moment.
